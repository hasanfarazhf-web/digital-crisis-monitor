How the analysis is done

Traffic strain
Weighted blend of jam factor, closure count, and disruption density.
Signal acceleration
Recent feed items are weighted by confidence and timing instead of simple volume.
Convergence watch score
Composite score combining transport friction and signal movement.

Deployment notes

Files to keep
Keep api/traffic.js, vercel.json, and .env.example unless you want separate backend changes.
Signals backend
This frontend expects your server or feed URL to supply JSON items to /api/signals.
Auto deploys
