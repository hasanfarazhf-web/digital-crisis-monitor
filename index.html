Scoring logic

Traffic strain

Combines average jam factor, closure ratio, and incident density normalized to the region footprint.

Signal acceleration

Measures velocity of matched posts or statements in the selected window, then weights them by source credibility and geo relevance.

Convergence watch score

Blends transport and narrative signals into a single 0 to 100 watch score with explainable weights.
